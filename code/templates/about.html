About Tourism Finder

Our Mission

Tourism Finder is dedicated to helping you discover the best tourist spots near you. Our goal is to make your travel planning seamless and enjoyable by providing you with personalized recommendations based on your preferences.

Features

  • Auto-complete search for locations
  • Top 5 nearest tourist spots
  • Language preferences
  • Translated descriptions and audio guides
  • Detailed information about starting locations
  • Personalized user experience with saved preferences and liked places
